Staff Distribution in Education Sectors

This dashboard provides insights into staff distribution across various education sectors in Australia for 2018. It breaks down staffing levels by state, sector (such as Catholic, public, or independent schools), and school type (primary or secondary). The data includes different staff roles, such as teaching, administrative, and specialist support staff, helping to analyze resource allocation and workforce composition within educational institutions.

Sales Dashboard

This visualization highlights key insights from hypothetical e-commerce data, showing sales (blue bars) and average customer ratings (green bars) across various product categories. Electronics has the highest sales, followed by Home & Garden, while Books show the lowest sales. Despite high sales, categories like Electronics don’t necessarily have the best ratings, whereas Toys have the highest average rating, and Clothing shows the lowest in terms of customer satisfaction.


Such analysis is valuable for e-commerce businesses to make informed decisions. It helps identify top-performing categories, understand customer satisfaction, and spot areas for improvement, like products with high sales but lower ratings. This data, when stored and analyzed in a MySQL database, can be used to generate even deeper insights, guiding strategies for inventory management, marketing, and customer service enhancements.
